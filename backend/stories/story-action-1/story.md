# Neon Shadows

The rain in Neo-Tokyo was acidic, sizzling against the carbon-fiber armor of Kael as he crouched on the gargoyle of the Synapse Corporation building. Below him, a dizzying drop into a sea of neon lights and flying vehicles.

His objective was simple: retrieve the Genesis Drive. The execution, however, would be anything but.

A squad of corporate enforcers patrolled the rooftop opposite to him. Kael's cybernetic eye flared to life, tagging multiple targets. He drew his plasma katana, the blade humming with lethal energy.

With a powerful leap, Kael soared across the chasm between the skyscrapers. He landed silently behind the first guard, dispatching him with a swift strike to the neural port. The second guard turned, raising a pulse rifle, but Kael was already moving. He slid under the line of fire, his blade slicing through the guard's weapon before delivering a stunning blow to the head.

Alarms blared, bathing the rooftop in harsh red light. Reinforcements poured from the stairwell. Kael smiled behind his mask. The real fight had just begun. He engaged his optical camouflage, disappearing into the neon shadows, ready to strike.
